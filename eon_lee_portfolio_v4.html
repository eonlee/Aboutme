About
THE FULL PICTURE

SooYeong (Eon) Lee is a Senior Manager of Global Experiential Marketing at Cheil Worldwide, Samsung's global marketing agency. Over 13 years, he has directed the full lifecycle of live events — from creative concept and stakeholder alignment through on-site execution — for esports tournaments, Olympic activations, developer conferences, and global product launches.

His work spans the Gamescom Samsung Gaming Festival, the Fortnite Korea Open, Samsung Galaxy Unpacked, and the Milano Cortina Winter Olympics Samsung House. He was awarded Professional of the Year at Cheil Worldwide in 2025.

A lifelong gamer since the Famicom era, Eon's approach to event production is rooted in player empathy — understanding what transforms a live moment into an unforgettable fan experience.
